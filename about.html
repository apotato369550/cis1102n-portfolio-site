Get to know our founder, our mission, and who this website was built for.

Who are We?

CompuFoundations is a dedicated project crafted by John Andre Yap, a BSCS 1 student who is passionate about Computer Science. This project was built in fulfillment for the requirements of the CIS 1102N Course. This platform brings together everything we’ve learned from our introductory course in computing, encompassing the core pillars of the field. Here, we cover foundational topics such as the essentials of computer science, number systems, data and information systems, as well as hardware, software, and the types of computers and motherboards that power our technology today.

Our Mission

Our mission at CompuFoundations is to make computer science accessible to everyone. We believe that anyone, regardless of their background, should have the opportunity to gain a solid foundation in this field. Through clear, well-structured content, we aim to empower students, professionals, and enthusiasts to deepen their knowledge and build skills in computing, promoting an inclusive learning experience for everyone who wants to explore computer science.

Who is this for?

CompuFoundations is designed for all curious minds—students who are just starting, professionals looking to strengthen their knowledge, and enthusiasts eager to explore computer science fundamentals. Our platform is built to foster a supportive environment where everyone, regardless of their experience, can benefit from accessible and quality computer science education. By diving into the basics, we aim to build a community that values continuous learning and growth.
